ABOUT US

Tech Ltd. was established and incorporated in the year 2008 to provide Project Management as general contractors in Civil and Electrical Works. Later the company diversified into water works and road works. The comapny does both Management and Consultancy.
Tech Ltd. is Tax Compliant and is registered with the National Construction Authority (NCA), therefore carries out its objectives with consideration of environment sustainability within NEMA policy and promotes the adoption of clean renewable energy.

PROJECTS

Over the years the company has been involved in various projects, such as:

    1. Building and civil works in Baringo and Kiambu County.
    2. Powerline construction for Kenya Power and lightning Company (KPLC) and Rural Electrification Authority (REA).
    3. Electrical and solar installation works for Baringo and Kiambu County.
    4. ISP Services - Windows Client Server, Backup Server, Primary Domain and ISP Webpage Hosting, Routing and Cabling for Fountain Technologies.
